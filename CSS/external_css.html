KIIT University

KIIT Bhubaneshwar is ranked 11th in Law Category and 34th in Overall Category by NIRF 2022. KIIT Bhubaneswar was established in 1992 as an Industrial Training Institute and In 2017, the institute was renamed Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology. KIIT is a private academic institution that offers manifold study programs at Undergraduate, Masters and Doctoral levels.
